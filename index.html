SUITFALL

A single-player card game of survival, arithmetic, and strategy.
Survive the falling suits as long as you can.

♠ Enemies ♦ Attacks ♣ Assists ♥ Health

Normal Mode

Resources are finite. Attacks, Assists & Health are discarded permanently. Survive to Round 10.

Infinite Mode

All used cards return to the deck. Play endlessly until you fall.

How to play ▾

The Basics

Draw 5 cards per round. Each round has a Player Phase then an Enemy Phase.

Player Phase

Defeat enemies (♠) by playing at least one Attack (♦), optionally boosted by Assists (♣), with a total value ≥ enemy health. Assists alone cannot defeat an enemy. Play Hearts (♥) to heal up to 20. Unused cards carry over to the next round.

Enemy Phase

Each undefeated enemy attacks you for its card value. Use Assists (♣) to block damage.

Damage Rules

Total attack + assist must meet or exceed enemy health to defeat it — and must include at least one ♦. Partial damage is ignored. Excess does not carry over.

Win Condition

Survive. In Normal Mode, reaching Round 10 is a victory. Infinite Mode ends only at HP 0.
